Our Roasting Philosophy

Sourcing

We build direct relationships with farmers and cooperatives. Every bean is traceable to its origin. We pay fair prices and visit farms annually.

Roasting

Small-batch roasting in-house. We profile each origin separately, testing dozens of roast curves to find the sweet spot where clarity meets complexity.

Quality Control

Every batch is cupped. We measure color, moisture, and density. Only beans that pass our standards make it into your cup.

Bring the ritual home

Our beans are available for purchase at all locations and through wholesale partnerships.
